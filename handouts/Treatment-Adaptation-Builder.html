What are you delivering, and which interventions?

Pick each approach in play (multi-select), then check the specific interventions you are actually using within it. Only those are carried through the fit and the handout, so the picture matches what you really do.

A treatment is not all-or-nothing. One intervention can fit the brain while the next does not. This tool checks the fit intervention by intervention.
